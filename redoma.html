    • DUAL MODE IT

      In finance industry, business is too critical to be migrated away from the mainframe or legacy stack. In the foreseeable future, they’re here to stay. For this reason, fin serv companies are embracing the Dual Mode IT: STABLE mode IT, for mature and established products; AGILE mode IT, characterized by microservices archiecture, scalable non-relational database, and iterative development process. Regardless how many modes of IT you have, the truth of data should hold only one version. Cladder helps you to distribute data from one mode to another, STABLE to AGILE or AGILE to STABLE.
